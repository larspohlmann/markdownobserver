Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

MarkdownObserver is a local-first macOS app for viewing and monitoring Markdown files. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

MarkdownObserver does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Your files stay on your device unless you choose to store them elsewhere.

File and Folder Access

The app may request access to files or folders you choose so it can read and monitor Markdown content. This access is used only for app functionality and is not used for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

Analytics and Tracking

MarkdownObserver does not use third-party analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, or advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

MarkdownObserver does not send your data to external servers as part of normal app use.

Children's Privacy

MarkdownObserver is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the project repository: GitHub Issues.
